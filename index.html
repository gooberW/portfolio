Nature Strikes Back

Originally developed during a Game Jam, Nature Strikes Back was later expanded into a full game to be presented at Lisbon Games Week 2025, featuring a mix of lighthearted humor and a classic B-movie aesthetic.

Players take on the role of "MAN", defending a city overrun by plant-monster hybrids born from nature's revenge.

After years of human impact on the environment, nature finally decides to fight back.

Wendwood

Wendwood is a first-person horror game developed as part of a university project.

The game tasks players with exploring a dense, atmospheric forest, solving environmental puzzles, and activating a series of mysterious obelisks to progress.

Players must navigate carefully, as the forest is patrolled by a relentless Wendigo, creating a tense and immersive survival experience.
